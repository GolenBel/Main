Decorations for the city.

The map contains small buildings made from micro blocks that can be used for your city, apartment, park.

Decorations for the city.

How to download the map.

1.Download the mods that are used on the map.

2.Unpack the archive with mods into the mods folder.

3.Download the map.

4.Unpack the archive with the map in the saves folder.

5.Install minecraft version 1.12.2.
